FAQ

Questions we get every week.

How long does carpet take to dry after steam cleaning?

With our Dry-Master system, as little as 4 hours — the shortest drying time in the industry. Standard truck-mount steam cleaning dries in 6–12 hours depending on airflow and humidity. We'll tell you the expected dry time before we start.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — every project, residential or commercial, starts with a free written estimate. Call (971) 344-1026 or use the chat assistant to start one now.

Can you really get pet urine odor out permanently?

Yes. The smell comes from urine crystals in the carpet pad, which surface cleaning can't reach. We use enzyme treatment plus hot-water extraction at the pad level to break them down.

Do you clean commercial buildings and warehouses?

That's a core part of our business: recurring janitorial contracts, commercial deep cleans, warehouse floor care, electrostatic disinfection, and post-construction final cleans.

What areas do you serve?

Gresham, Portland, Troutdale, Fairview, Wood Village, Happy Valley, Clackamas, Sandy, Damascus, Milwaukie, and the surrounding Portland metro.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Cascade Complete Cleaning Service, Inc. is fully licensed and insured for residential and commercial work in Oregon. We accept credit cards.
