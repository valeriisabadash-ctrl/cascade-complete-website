FAQ

Questions we get every week.

How long does carpet take to dry after steam cleaning?

With the Dry-Master system, carpets can dry in as little as 4 hours. Standard truck-mounted steam cleaning dries in 6 to 12 hours depending on airflow and humidity.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Residential and commercial projects start with a free written estimate. Call (971) 344-1026 or use the quote form.

Can you remove pet odor?

Yes. The team uses enzyme treatment plus hot-water extraction to break down odor at the source. Severe cases may require pad treatment.

Do you clean commercial buildings and warehouses?

Yes. Services include recurring janitorial contracts, commercial deep cleans, warehouse floor care, electrostatic disinfection and post-construction final cleans.

What areas do you serve?

Gresham, Portland, Troutdale, Fairview, Wood Village, Happy Valley, Clackamas, Sandy, Damascus, Milwaukie and the surrounding Portland metro.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Cascade Complete Cleaning Services Inc. is licensed and insured for residential and commercial work in Oregon.
